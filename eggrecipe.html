Eggs are one of my favorites foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs

ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Methoood

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over medium heat
  2. mix eggs and cream in bowl
  3. once the butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds
  5. when the eggs are still moist remove them from the heat
